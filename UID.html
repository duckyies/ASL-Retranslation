Smart Home Automation

Smart home automation systems are becoming increasingly popular, offering users enhanced control over their home environment through interconnected devices. This paper presents a novel approach to smart home automation using linked lists, a fundamental data structure, to efficiently manage and control various home appliances. In this system, each home appliance is represented as a node in a linked list, allowing for dynamic management of devices.

The linked list structure enables easy addition, removal, and modification of appliances, as well as real-time adjustments to their states. By leveraging the properties of linked lists, the system can scale with increasing numbers of devices while maintaining efficiency.

ASL Translator

The American Sign Language (ASL) is a crucial mode of communication. This paper proposes an innovative approach to translating ASL into text or speech using a hybrid model that combines Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs).

The CNN extracts spatial features from ASL hand gesture images, while the RNN captures the sequential dependencies. Experimental results demonstrate the efficacy of this model in achieving high accuracy.
